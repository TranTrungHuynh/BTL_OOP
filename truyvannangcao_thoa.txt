1. Những sự kiện nào được quản lý bởi tổ chức BlackBerry và được chuẩn bị ở đất nước Cape Verde
PREFIX country: <http://shadow.org/country/>
PREFIX organization: <http://shadow.org/organization/>
PREFIX relationship: <http://shadow.org/relationship/>
PREFIX event: <http://shadow.org/event/>
	SELECT ?object
	WHERE {
    ?event event:has_Label ?object.
    ?event relationship:managed ?organization .
  	?organization organization:has_Label "BlackBerry" .
    ?event relationship:prepared ?country .
    ?country country:has_Label "Cape Verde" .
}

2. Những người tham gia sự kiện Vietbuild Home diễn ra ở Seattle, United States
PREFIX person: <http://shadow.org/person/>
PREFIX relationship: <http://shadow.org/relationship/>
PREFIX event: <http://shadow.org/event/>
PREFIX location: <http://shadow.org/location/>
	SELECT ?object
	WHERE {
    ?person person:has_Label ?object.
    ?person relationship:practice ?event .
    ?event event:has_Label "Vietbuild Home" .
    ?event relationship:take_Place_Of ?location.
    ?location location:has_Label "Seattle, United States" .
}

3. Tổ chức Samsung Group tổ chức những sựu kiện nào vào Feb. 21, 1993
PREFIX event: <http://shadow.org/event/>
PREFIX relationship: <http://shadow.org/relationship/>
PREFIX organization: <http://shadow.org/organization/>
PREFIX time: <http://shadow.org/time/>
	SELECT ?object
	WHERE {
	?organization organization:has_Label "Samsung Group".
    ?organization relationship:organization ?event.
    ?event event:has_Label ?object.
    ?event relationship:take_Place_On ?time.
    ?time time:has_Label "Feb. 21, 1993".
	}
